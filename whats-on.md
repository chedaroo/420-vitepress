# What's on?

Come join us for a knees-up at my home near Llangollen, Rhysgog Farm.

Nestled in the Dee valley, its handy location, awesome features and epic vistas make it an ideal venue for a party.

This year, the event will run the full weekend — _Friday 7th to Monday 10th August 2026_.

## ☀️ Day

During the day, you're encouraged to use us as a base to explore North Wales. Whether you're into climbing, riding, paddling, walking, running or even just boozing in pubs, there's loads to do.

For those who'd prefer to stay on site, there will be games, activities, music and the chance to play in the river.

## 🌙 Night

At night, it's party time! The barn will be filled with decor, lighting, music and dancing into the small hours — and there will of course be the obligatory campfire.

## ⛺️ Accommodation

Stay over — we have plenty of space for tents and vans, with standard campsite facilities. Not into camping? The Chainbridge Hotel is an easy walk away (~1.2km).

## 🍺 Food & Drink

Feel free to bring your own food and drink (no glass please).

There will also be a reasonably priced licensed bar with ice cold draft beer, cider and spirits.

Food will be available in the evening. I'm not sure exactly what yet — last year was burgers and brats (plus vegan versions), the year before was beef chilli (plus vegan version).

## 👶 Family Friday

I have a family, most of you have met them — I'm very proud of them and I love them very much.

If you have a family, I'd love to meet them too. A bunch of mini yous? Awesome!

Friday is the day for this — everything planned will be suitable for all ages, including the evening entertainment.

## 💪 Sprog-Free Saturday

Some things are better without kids, and relaxing is one of them.

If you've brought your family but plan to stay for the main party on Saturday night, you'll need to arrange for a friend or grandparent to take them off your hands, or have them picked up before the evening.

This may change in future years, but for now — this is the way.

I'm keen for feedback on this, so if you have any thoughts please let me know. I'm all ears.

## 🎟️ Tickets

As much as we'd love to bankroll the whole thing, it just isn't sustainable year on year.

So you'll need a super duper reasonably priced [ticket](/tickets) to come along.

All tickets include camping and full use of the facilities — cheaper than any other local campsite, and it's a party too!

If you really can't afford a ticket, please message us and we'll work something out.

**Please don't wait — get your tickets as early as you can.**

It really helps us plan and budget when we know who's coming in advance. Thank you ☺️
